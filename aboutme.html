SPE ABUAD is a sub of SPE Benin section which is a sub of SPE Nigeria Council which is also a sub of SPE International, one of the best or the best engineering and oil/gas organization in the world if I do say so myself. I went ahead to volunteer to be the Secretary General of SPE ABUAD in 2022 and these positions have thought me so much and have opened doors of opportunites for me. I currently volunteer for SPE Lagos section. As a Girl in STEM, Because of these, I was nominated to receive a Girl in STEM award given by SPE Nigeria Council.
